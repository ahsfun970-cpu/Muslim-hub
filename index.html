Quran Reflections

I’m not a hafiz. These are verses that hit me. Read full Quran with translation from trusted sites.

Read Complete Quran: quran.com | surah.pk Urdu

Verses I Highlighted

1. Surah Asr 103:1-3
English: By time, indeed mankind is in loss, except those who believe, do good, advise truth, advise patience.
Urdu: Waqt ki qasam, insan nuqsan mein hai, siwaye unke jo iman laye, naik amal kiye, haq ki naseehat ki, sabr ki naseehat ki.
My note: 4 kaam = nijaat. I keep this on my desk.
2. Surah Aal-e-Imran 3:103
English: Hold to the rope of Allah all together and do not become divided.
Urdu: Allah ki rassi ko sab mil kar mazbooti se thaam lo aur tafriqa na dalo.
My note: Unity is command. Division is loss.
3. Surah Al-Baqarah 2:286
English: Allah does not burden a soul beyond what it can bear.
Urdu: Allah kisi jaan par uski taaqat se zyada bojh nahi daalta.
My note: When I’m stressed, I read this.
4. Surah Ash-Sharh 94:6
English: Indeed, with hardship comes ease.
Urdu: Beshak mushkil ke saath aasani hai.
My note: Twice repeated. Allah is telling us.
5. Surah Al-Ikhlas 112:1-4
English: Say, He is Allah, One. Allah, the Eternal Refuge...
Urdu: Kaho, Wo Allah aik hai...
My note: I read this 3 times before sleep.

More verses coming InshaAllah. For tafsir, read Ibn Kathir or ask an alim.

Hadees on Unity

Saheeh references only. I am learning, not teaching.

1. Hold to the Jamaah
The Prophet ﷺ said: "I enjoin you to have taqwa of Allah, and to hear and obey... and hold fast to the Jamaah."
Reference: Abu Dawood 4607, Tirmidhi 2676 - Saheeh
2. The Saved Group
The Prophet ﷺ said: "The Jews split into 71 groups, Christians into 72, and my Ummah will split into 73. All are in the Fire except one." They asked: Which one? He said: "That which follows what I and my Companions are upon today."
Reference: Abu Dawood 4597, Tirmidhi 2641 - Hasan Saheeh

My note: The way out is Sunnah + Sahaba. Not names, not arguments. Follow Rasoolullah ﷺ.
